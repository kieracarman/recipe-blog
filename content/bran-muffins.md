---
title: Bran Muffins
---
*makes 24 muffins*

**Preheat oven to 400F.**

**Combine and let water absorb:**
1 cup wheat bran
1 cup boiling water

**Whisk together:**
310g flour
12g tsp baking soda
3g salt

**Beat thoroughly:**
2 eggs
200g brown sugar

**Beat in:**
Prepared hydrated wheat bran
2 cups wheat bran
120g grapeseed oil
460g buttermilk

**Add to dry mix and stir until well blended.**

**Bake 15 minutes.**
