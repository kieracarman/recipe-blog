# Scones

**Preheat oven to 400F.**

**Whisk together:**
250g flour
65g sugar
1 tbsp baking powder
1/2 tsp salt
*Optional: mix-ins as desired (fruit, nuts, cinnamon chips)*

**Grate cold into dry mix:**
85g (3/4 stick) butter

**In 2 cup Pyrex, whisk:**
1/2 cup heavy cream
1 egg
1 tsp vanilla

**Combine wet and dry mixes, stir until just combined. Should be crumbly!**

**Scoop onto parchment-lined baking sheet, shape into 7 inch disk. Cut into 8 equal sectors and spread out on pan. Brush with heavy cream.**

**Bake for 18-22 minutes, until browned and cooked through.**
