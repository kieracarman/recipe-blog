---
title: Chocolate Crinkle Cookies
---
*makes 20-22 cookies*

**Beat together:**
113g (1 stick) butter
100g sugar
100g brown sugar

**Add and beat until combined:**
1 egg
1 tsp vanilla

**In a separate bowl, whisk:**
125g flour
51g cocoa powder
1 tsp baking soda
1/2 tsp salt

**Mix wet and dry ingredients. Chill dough thoroughly.**

**Roll dough into balls, roll in powdered sugar.**

**Bake for 11-12 minutes.**
