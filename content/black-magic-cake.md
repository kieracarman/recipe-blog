---
title: Black Magic Cake
---

(makes two 9" round cake layers)

**Preheat oven to 350°F.**

**Beat until ribbon-y:**
400g sugar
2 eggs

**Whisk together, then emulsify into sugar and eggs:**
1/2 cup olive oil
1 cup black coffee
1 cup buttermilk

**Sift in:**
220g flour
95g cocoa
12g baking soda
3g baking powder
5g salt

**Stir to combine.**

**Pour into greased and lined 9” round cake pans and bake for 30-40 minutes.**
