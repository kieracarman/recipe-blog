---
title: Buttermilk Biscuits
---
*makes 12 large biscuits*

**Preheat oven to 450F.**

**Whisk together:**
500g flour
30g baking powder

**Grate cold into dry mix:**
170g (1.5 sticks) butter

**Add and mix until just combined:**
2 cups buttermilk

**Turn out onto heavily floured counter.**

**With hands, flatten dough, cover with flour, then fold and flatten again. Do this a few times to create layers, applying flour liberally.**

**Cut into round biscuits, gathering and re-flattening dough scraps to cut again to make 12 biscuits in total.**

**Bake for 15 minutes, until lightly browned.**
