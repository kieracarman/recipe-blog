---
title: Chicago-style Thin Crust Pizza
---

(makes 2 pizzas - 16")

## Dough

300g flour
150g water
30g oil
7g salt
7g sugar
1g yeast

## Sauce

1 jar passata
3oz tomato paste
1 tbsp olive oil
1 tbsp green herbs
1/2 tbsp sugar
1 tsp garlic powder
1 tsp salt

**Preheat oven to 500F with pizza stone for 30-60 minutes.**
